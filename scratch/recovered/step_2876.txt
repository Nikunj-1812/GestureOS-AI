10: ## Technical Architecture & Design
11: 
12: ### 1. Drawing State & Gesture Control
13: * **Hovering vs. Drawing State**:
14:   * **Hovering (Hover state)**: When the hand is detected but the user is not pinching, the cursor moves a visual drawing pointer (dot) around the canvas.
15:   * **Drawing (Draw state)**: When the user pinches (Thumb + Index distance $\le$ click threshold), a drawing stroke is active. Lines connect successive coordinate points.
16:   * **Idle**: When no hand is detected in the camera frame.
17: * **Undo & Redo**:
18:   * Maintain a list of strokes: `self.strokes = list[list[tuple[int, int, str, int]]]` representing the brush coordinates, colors, and sizes.
19:   * Maintain a redo stack: `self.redo_stack = list[list[tuple[int, int, str, int]]]`.
20: * **Brush Settings**:
21:   * **Color Palette**: Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Purple, White.
22:   * **Brush Size**: Small (2px), Medium (5px), Large (10px), Extra Large (20px).
