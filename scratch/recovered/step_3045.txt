41: ## Technical Details
42: 
43: ### Analytics Calculations
44: * **Words Per Minute (WPM)**:
45:   $$\text{WPM} = \frac{\text{len(Text)} / 5}{\text{ElapsedTime (Minutes)}}$$
46:   *(Elapsed time is clamped to a minimum of 5 seconds to prevent initial divisor spikes).*
47: * **Accuracy Percentage**:
48:   $$\text{Accuracy} = \max\left(0, \frac{\text{Keystrokes} - 2 \times \text{Backspaces}}{\text{Keystrokes}} \times 100\right)$$
49:   *(Treats Backspace keypresses as correction cycles that require a deletion keystroke plus a correction keystroke).*
